## Rest, Restore, Reconnect

The grounds of Moulin a Reves were made for slowing down. Whether you want a guided yoga session on the morning lawn, a deep-tissue massage in the privacy of your home, or simply a quiet afternoon by the millstream, we can arrange it.

### Yoga & Movement

Our local yoga instructor offers private and group sessions on the lawn or in the barn space. Morning flow, gentle stretch, or dynamic vinyasa -- sessions are tailored to your group's level and mood. Mats and props provided.

### Massage & Bodywork

Licensed therapists come to you. Choose from Swedish, deep tissue, sports massage, or aromatherapy. Treatments can be set up in your living room, on the terrace, or in the garden pavilion.

### Beauty & Self-Care

Facials, manicures, and pedicures by appointment. All products are organic and locally sourced. Perfect for a pre-celebration pamper session or simply because you deserve it.

### Pricing

Yoga sessions from 60 EUR per group. Massage treatments from 80 EUR per person. Beauty services quoted individually. Book in advance to ensure availability during your stay.
