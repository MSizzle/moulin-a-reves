## Let Us Feed You

One of the great pleasures of staying at Moulin a Reves is not having to think about meals. Our network of local chefs and caterers can handle everything from a simple breakfast delivery to a five-course celebration dinner under the stars.

### Breakfast Baskets

Start each morning with a basket of fresh croissants, artisan bread, local butter and jam, fruit, and freshly squeezed juice delivered to your door. Available daily or for your entire stay.

### Private Chef Dinners

Our private chefs source ingredients from the local markets and prepare multi-course meals in your kitchen or outdoors at the long table. Menus are tailored to your group's preferences, dietary needs, and the season.

### Special Occasions

Planning a birthday, anniversary, or milestone celebration? We coordinate with florists, musicians, and event planners to create unforgettable evenings in the garden.

### Pricing

Breakfast baskets from 15 EUR per person. Private chef dinners from 65 EUR per person. Special occasion packages quoted individually. Contact us for details.
