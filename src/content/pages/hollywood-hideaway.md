## Description

This historic mill complex is the ultimate getaway to write your novel, finish your screenplay, paint your masterpiece and relax in the beautiful French countryside.

For 60 years, The Hollywood Hideaway was a treasured sanctuary getaway for Hollywood's #1 power lawyer whose clients included Tom Cruise, Dustin Hoffman, Michael Jackson, Mario Puzzo and the luminaries of LA.

This was his happy home where he entertained his Hollywood pals and cooked and relaxed in the French countryside.

Now you can follow in the footsteps of the stars and make this your own home in France. It is beautifully furnished with French antiques.

**The Secret Garden**
- Adjacent sitting room suite with pull-out sofa for 1 person
