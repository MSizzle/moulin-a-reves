## Description

Come to France and be inspired! This beautiful, sunny cottage will inspire you to write your masterpiece, finish your screenplay, have good conversations. Perfect for a family or 2 couples.

## Rooms

### Upstairs

**1. La Chambre des Artistes (The Artists' Room)**
- King bed, large bedroom
- Twin bed + sink in room

**2. La Chambre des Écrivains (The Writers' Room)**
- Double bed + sink in room

Bedrooms share a bathroom with a tub/shower located in the hall.

## Entertaining Spaces

### Living Room
- Spacious, sun-drenched living room with 2 couches
- Decorated with fine antiques and art, including an authentic Louis Vuitton trunk as a coffee table
- Beautiful fireplace (non-working)
- Huge French doors open into the view of the stream and apple trees
- Offers wonderful light for inspiration

### Dining Room
- Elegant dining room with a table for up to 18 people

### Kitchen
- Eat-in kitchen
- Oven, full-size refrigerator and freezer
- Microwave, toaster, Nespresso maker, electric kettle
