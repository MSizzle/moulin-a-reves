## Our Story

Moulin a Reves began as a dream shared over a long dinner in the French countryside. What started as a crumbling mill and two neglected outbuildings has become a place where families and friends gather to slow down, reconnect, and make memories.

The restoration took the better part of a decade, guided by respect for the original stonework and a belief that old buildings deserve a second life. Every beam was cleaned by hand, every shutter painted the same shade of blue that the previous owners had chosen a century ago.

## The Place

Set along a gentle millstream on the edge of the Fontainebleau forest, the compound sits in one of the most beautiful corners of the Ile-de-France. The village of Barbizon, famous for its painters, is a short walk away. Paris is an hour by train.

## The Owners

We are a family who fell in love with this place and want to share it with others. We live nearby and are always available to help with recommendations, logistics, or simply to drop off a bottle of wine from the local vineyard.
