## Description

Dreams come true at Moulin à Rêves, a beautiful historic mill house in Mereville, the watercress capital of France.

This beautifully renovated and decorated historic stone home built in 1800 was a working mill — in fact the Juine River flows right through the house! This 3-story home is a comfortable refuge for 12 guests.

Relax in the gardens and watch as the day goes by. Let your worries float away on the Juine River.

Wake up to the roosters crowing in the chicken coop.

Go back in time as you gaze at the flower gardens and Giverny-style bridge.

Make a wish in the wishing well!

Dine al fresco with friends, colleagues, family or students.

This is a wonderful compound for inspiration, fun, reflection and quiet time together.

## Rooms

### Ground Floor

**1. La Chambre de la Rivière (River Room)**
- 2 twin beds
- Bathroom in the hall with a shower

### Upstairs

**2. La Suite Romantique**
- King bed, large master bedroom
- En-suite bathroom with a shower

**3. Les Cerises (The Cherry Room)**
- Queen bed
- Sink and bidet in room

**4. Les Roses (The Rose Room)**
- 2 twin beds
- Rooms 3 and 4 share a bathroom in the hall with a bidet and clawfoot bathtub

### Second Floor Attic

**5. Le Loft Suite**
- 2 twin beds (can be combined as a King) in a large bedroom
- A large separate room with a big TV; the couch can be set up as 2 twin beds on the floor
- These 2 rooms share a bathroom with a shower in the hall
- Note: This room requires climbing a steep flight of stairs
